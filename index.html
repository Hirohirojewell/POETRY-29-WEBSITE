Beneath the glow of twinkling lights,

We danced through cold December nights.

Each snowflake kissed your rosy cheeks,

A love so pure, no words could speak.

Amid the cheer of carol chimes,

Our hearts entwined through wintertime.

Your warmth defied December’s chill,

A magic only love could fill.

Under mistletoe, we found our bliss,

The sweetest gift, our first soft kiss.

And in your arms, the world felt bright,

A perfect love on Christmas night.

So here I stand, a vow to make,

This love will never bend nor break.

Through every season, far or near,

I’ll hold you close, my Christmas dear.

Prayer

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for the gift of love we share,

For the warmth that fills our hearts this Christmas.

May this season strengthen our bond,

And fill our lives with peace and joy.

Let us grow together in faith and kindness,

Guided by Your light through all our days.

In Your holy name, Amen.
